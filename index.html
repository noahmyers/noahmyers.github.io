Speaking & Workshops

I also offer occasional talks and workshops for professional audiences (CPE groups, firms, and student organizations) on auditing, technology, and applied AI in accounting. These sessions are research-informed and practical, typically using concrete cases and demonstrations to help audiences understand how emerging tools and information environments affect accounting work and assurance. Details on formats and sample topics are available on the Speaking & Workshops page.

Of Good Report

Of Good Report is a media-literacy project I built that catalogs journalism and public sources cited by leaders of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. My goal is to encourage better media literacy, wider reading, and better information habits without implying endorsement of any outlet.
